Board Member Recruitment

Ascend at UCLA is recruiting for board members! The available positions are:

1. Vice President of Student Relations
2. Vice President of Marketing
3. Chair of Professional Development
4. Chair of Community Engagement
5. Chair of Finance
6. Chair of marketing

Each position will be elected and held for one academic year. You will work under the direct mentorship of the executive board and develop critical professional and leadership skills that will set you apart from your peers. The deadline to submit the application is Friday, April 1st, 11:59 PM, and you will be notified during the weekend if you are selected for an interview.

Underclassmen are encouraged to apply as well, and we look forward to seeing your application!

Ascend is the largest, non-profit Pan-Asian organization for business professionals in North America. Ascend reaches 60,000 people with 40+ student chapters and 18 professional chapters located in both the United States and Canada around major business hubs and educational institutions. Familyship Recruitment

We're so excited to see everybody in person this year, and thrilled to announce Ascend's social "Familyship" program for Fall Quarter 2021!!

You'll be put into a group of people with two family leads who'll coordinate fun socials such as game nights, off campus adventures, study sessions, eat out, etc. We will also host events for all the families to come together and reveal your family on our kick off event!

Expectations:
- Attend Ascend Family kick off event to meet your family!
- Attend at least 3 family socials per quarter
- Stay in touch with your family/family lead
- $15 family fee for the whole year

Benefits:
- Prizes for the best family at the end of the fall quarter
- Opportunity for peer mentorship, such as unlimited 1:1 sessions with board officers
- Invitation to company sponsored events, only exclusive to family members (such as field trips to sponsoring companies)
- Prioritized access to in-person socials
- Create a tight-knit community and friendships with your family!

Familyship Application is closed for Fall quarter, but winter application will come soon!
